Grid

Column classes Applicable dimensions
.c-1...c-12 From 360px device width and up (mobile)
.cl-1...cl-12 From 720px device width and up (desktop)

On devices under 360px wide each column is rendered on a separate row.

An optional.has-gand.has-glclass can be applied on the.rowto enable gutters on that row, for that size.

Gutters are 1 rem wide, split evenly between non-first-child and non-last-child columns.
