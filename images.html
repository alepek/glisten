Images

The images library of glisten contain some simple styling for thumbnails as well as larger images.

Thumbnails

Thumbnails come in three sizes:120x120180x180240x240:

Thumbnails are used by wrapping animgin.img-thumband one of these size classes:.is-s.is-m.is-l

Images are resized to fit within their container. Thumbnails require rectangular images to not distort the image ratio.
